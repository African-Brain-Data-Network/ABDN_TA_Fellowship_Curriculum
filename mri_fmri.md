# MRI / fMRI Curriculum (ABDN TA)

## Overview
Focus: Brain Imaging & Functional Mapping

---

## June — Foundations
**Learning Objective:** Understand MRI physics and brain anatomy  

- Week 1: Basics of Brain Anatomy  
- Week 2: What is MRI?  
- Week 3: Magnetic Fields & Signals  
- Week 4: Image Formation

## July — MRI Acquisition
**Learning Objective:** Understand how MRI/fMRI data is collected  

- Week 1: MRI Sequences (T1, T2)

- Week 4: Safety and Ethics

## August — Data Analysis

- Week 3: Preprocessing pipelines  
- Week 4: Statistical Analysis

## September — Paper Writing

- Week 4: Results & Discussion

## October — Finalizing Paper
