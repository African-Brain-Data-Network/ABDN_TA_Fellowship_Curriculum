# EEG Curriculum (ABDN TA)

## Overview
**Focus Timeline:**
- June: Introductions
- July: EEG Signals & Recording
- August: Data Analysis & Tools
- September: Paper Writing
- October: Finalizing Paper

---

## June — Introductions
**Learning Objective:** Understand brain basics and what EEG measures

- Week 1: Introduction to the Brain and Neurons  
- Week 2: What is EEG?  
- Week 3: Action Potential and Signal Creation  
- Week 4: Waveforms  
- Week 5: Group Discussions  

---

## July — EEG Signals & Recording
**Learning Objective:** Understand Practical Approaches to EEG  

- Week 1: Electrodes, caps, wearable EEG  
- Week 2: Signal vs Noise  
- Week 3: Experimental Design  
- Week 4: Ethics in EEG  

---

## August — Data Analysis & Tools
**Learning Objective:** Understand steps to analyzing real EEG data  

- Week 1: Intro to EEG analysis tools and datasets  
- Week 2: MNE-Python and EEGLAB  
- Week 3: Signal Processing  
- Week 4: Event-Related Potentials  
- Week 5: Activity — Load dataset and plot first brainwave  

---

## September — Paper Writing

- Week 1: Epilepsy in Africa  
- Week 2: Psychosis and Mental Health  
- Week 3: Literature Review Writing  
- Week 4: Structuring a Scientific Paper  

---

## October — Finalizing Paper

- Week 1: Draft Review  
- Week 2: Editing and Feedback  
- Week 3: Final Submission Prep  
- Week 4: Presentation Practice
